Video - Brazil / 2002, 2003		  

Director of Outside Shoot, Production and Interviews
Raquel Boechat 

Documentary in video on the restoration work on the historic building built in the 18th century which has housed the Paraty Cultural Centre since 2003, in the historic town in the south of Rio de Janeiro state.

The restoration teams were filmed at work and the engineers, carpenters, labourers, architects and historians, natives and inhabitants of the town, including some who used to go there, yeas ago, when it was used as a dance club, and show us the challenges faced and the techniques used in the restoration, along with a few other curiosities.

The documentary accompanied each stage of the restoration, from that of abandonment to the completely restored house, which today houses the áudio-visual archive of one of the most important historical buildings of the brazilian colonial period and these days a meeting-point for people and culture in this country.

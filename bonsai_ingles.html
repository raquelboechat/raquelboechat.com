The film   

Written and produced by artistic director Cristian Machado and journalist Raquel Boechat, “Introduction to Bonsai” is a video course which is notable for the quality of its production:
Photography – recorded in Digital Betacam, the most modern video technology then available in the world;
Soundtrack – especially composed by Ricardo Pontes;
Original Script – makes the educational work attractive to both laypeople, the curious and for those with some knowledge of the matter;
Presented by – Carlos Tramujas; classified in the International Competition "TAIKANTEN 99" (Japan/1999), consultant in Brazil and Europe and a reference on Bonsai in South America.

The cultivation of Bonsai, for most people, is still shrouded in mystery, “that tiny tree, very sensitive and which dies easily”.

In order to demystify this centuries-old art , “Introduction to Bonsai” takes a detailed look at  the techniques to produce real bonsai, tips on care, the history of the art and its introduction to Brazil, with more than 40 examples from the artists’ collection.

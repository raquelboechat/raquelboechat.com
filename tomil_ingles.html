Tomil Gonçalves

TV Artistic Director, with a Degree in Theatre from university UNI-RIO, Tomil Gonçalves has worked on productions for open and cable TV stations in the fields of drama, events and children’s programming since the 1980s.

He has directed soap operas, musical programs and theatre for the Ministry of Culture, and scientific documentaries sponsored by health institutions.

As an actor, he has appeared in important theatre productions, such as "Rasga Coração", "As Gralhas” and was a member of the original cast of "Saltimbancos", by Chico Buarque de Hollanda.

Today he works on directing audio-visual programs on history, sciences and pedagogy for teachers and students in the Rio de Janeiro school system.

With a number of international prizes to his name the director was nominated for the competitive “22 International Television Science Program Festival”, at the EiffelTower in Paris, Autumn 2006.

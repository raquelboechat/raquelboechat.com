 The film

 “Espelho D’Água – Uma Viagem no Rio São Francisco”, (Water surface, a Voyage down the São Francisco River)  by first-time filmmaker Marcus Vinicius Cezar was produced by Carla Camurati and features actors Fabio Assunção, Carla Regina, Francisco Carvalho, Charles Paraventi, Aramis Trindade, José Ricardo and Analu Tavares.
Copacabana Filmes, Brazil / 2004

During a voyage by a carioca girl to find her boyfriend, a photographer who disappeared whilst going down the River São Francisco, the history and legends of the River are revealed.

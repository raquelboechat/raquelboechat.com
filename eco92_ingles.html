Series of three vídeos on 400 years of history and culture in Rio de Janeiro, especially made for the Rio City Hall “Rio Eco 92” Exhibition at the Imperial Palace.

The event was part of the official programme of the 1992 Rio de Janeiro World Summit, or ECO-92, as the United Nation Conference on Environment and Development is better known. The objective of the conference was to reconcile development with the preservation of ecosystems and was attended by the heads of state and heads of governments of almost 180 countries.
